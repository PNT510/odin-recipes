Tacos

Description

What is there to say about white people tacos? They're the best. You don't need no onions or any of that stuff. Just some meat cheese and taco sauce

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Shreded Cheese
  • Taco Sauce

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef.
  2. Add Taco seasoning and water.
  3. Simmer.
  4. Assemble.
